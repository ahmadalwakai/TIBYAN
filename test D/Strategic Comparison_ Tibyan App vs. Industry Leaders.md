# Strategic Comparison: Tibyan App vs. Industry Leaders

This comparison evaluates the current state of **Tibyan App** against the established benchmarks of **Roya Academy** (Academic/Scholarly focus) and **Mindvalley** (Transformation/High-Engagement focus).

| Feature | Tibyan App (Current) | Roya Academy (Benchmark) | Mindvalley (Benchmark) | **Gap Analysis** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hero Section** | Standard text + stock photo. | High-profile scholar images + prestige branding. | Cinematic video + transformation-driven copy. | Needs higher-end, "exclusive" visuals and a more compelling hook. |
| **Trust & Authority** | General stats (13k students). | Endorsements from top scholars (Dr. Tariq Suwaidan, etc.). | Celebrity instructors + "5x completion rate" claim. | Missing high-profile "Face of the Brand" or specific expert authority. |
| **Methodology** | Mentions "Academic Commitment." | Clear 3-tier system (Course -> Diploma -> Program). | "Quest" model + 6-phase meditation methodology. | Methodology is mentioned but not visually or structurally "branded." |
| **Lead Generation** | "Explore Programs" (Standard). | Detailed syllabus + Scholar bios. | Free Masterclass / Assessment (High conversion). | Lacks a "Free Entry Point" that delivers immediate value. |
| **User Experience** | Modern, clean, dark mode. | Traditional, scholarly, authoritative. | Gamified, app-first, highly immersive. | Tibyan has the best modern UI but lacks the "Immersive Experience" of Mindvalley. |
| **Social Proof** | Basic rating (3.6â˜…). | Deep testimonials from known intellectuals. | Video testimonials + massive community "Tribes." | Needs deeper, story-driven testimonials rather than just star ratings. |

## Key Insights for Tibyan App
1.  **Authority Gap:** While Tibyan is technically sound, it lacks the "Gravitas" of Roya Academy. Users trust Roya because of the *people* behind it. Tibyan needs to highlight its instructors as "Rockstars" in their fields.
2.  **Engagement Gap:** Mindvalley succeeds because it's "Addictive." Tibyan's "Academic Commitment" is a great USP, but it should be framed as a **Challenge** or **Quest** to make it more engaging.
3.  **Visual Gap:** The current visuals are "Safe." To be "The Bestest," the design needs to move from "Standard EdTech" to "Premium Academy."
